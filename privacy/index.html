PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 15, 2026

OVERVIEW

Grimoire is a recipe management app developed by Slingblade Studios. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Grimoire, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account
  • Recipe data — the recipes, ingredients, and preparation steps you create and save within the app
  • Character profile — your in-app hero name and character selection

We do not collect your real name, phone number, location, payment information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To create and manage your Grimoire account
  • To store and retrieve your recipes across sessions and devices
  • To display your character profile and progress within the app

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Firebase is a Google service and your data is stored on Google's infrastructure. You can review Google's data practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

DATA SHARING

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party service that processes your data is Google Firebase, used solely to operate the app.

DATA RETENTION & DELETION

Your data is retained for as long as you maintain an active Grimoire account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the app:

  • Go to Account in the bottom navigation
  • Tap Delete Account and confirm with your password

Upon deletion, all your recipes, profile data, and account credentials are permanently removed from our systems.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Grimoire is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
